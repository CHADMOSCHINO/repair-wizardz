About WECARE Jamaica

Who We Are

WECARE Jamaica is a U.S.-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to providing rapid humanitarian aid and long-term rebuilding to Jamaican families affected by natural disasters.

We recover. We rebuild. We restore hope.

Our Mission

To deliver immediate relief to families devastated by disasters and support the long-term rebuilding of communities across Jamaica.

Our Vision

We envision a Jamaica where every family has a safe home, strong community support, and hope for a better future — even after the worst storms.

Our Story

Born from Crisis, Built on Hope

In 2025, Hurricane Melissa tore through Jamaica, leaving thousands without homes, food, or basic necessities. The destruction was overwhelming — families lost everything in a single night.

Our founders saw the devastation and knew one thing:

"We could not wait for help. We had to be the help."

From that moment, WECARE Jamaica was born — starting as an emergency response initiative, and growing into a long-term rebuilding movement.
