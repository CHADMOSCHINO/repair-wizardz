Current Emergency: Hurricane Melissa Relief

Rebuilding Jamaica,
One Family at a Time.

WECARE Jamaica is a U.S.-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit providing rapid humanitarian aid, disaster relief, and long-term community rebuilding for families impacted by Hurricane Melissa and future natural disasters.

We believe in unity, community strength, and restoring hope after devastation.

Hurricane Melissa devastated thousands of families across Jamaica. Homes were destroyed. Roads collapsed. Communities were cut off. WECARE Jamaica responded immediately — providing food, water, hygiene kits, medical supplies, and boots-on-the-ground support.

Our Mission

Fast, compassionate aid. Long-term community rebuilding.

At WECARE Jamaica, our mission is simple: provide fast, effective, and compassionate aid to families affected by disasters — and help rebuild the communities they call home.

We believe in unity, community strength, and restoring hope after devastation.

What We Do

Emergency Relief & Long-Term Recovery

From rapid response to rebuilding, WECARE Jamaica walks with families every step of the way.

Emergency Relief

Delivering food, water, clothing, hygiene kits, medical supplies, and critical logistics to affected families.

Long-Term Recovery

Rebuilding homes, restoring schools, providing tools, and supporting families as they restart their lives.

Medical & Health Support

Sending medical supplies, volunteer nurses & doctors, and first aid teams to communities in crisis.
