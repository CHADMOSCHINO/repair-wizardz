Our Programs

How We Help Jamaica

We operate across three major pillars to support Jamaica's recovery and future resilience.

Program 1

Emergency Relief

Providing immediate support to families impacted by natural disasters.

We Deliver:

  • Food & water
  • Clothing
  • Hygiene kits
  • Medical supplies
  • Disaster-response logistics
  • Rapid on-ground support teams

Our emergency response model ensures supplies reach affected communities fast, regardless of road conditions, power loss, or communications breakdown.

Emergency supplies in action

Rebuilding communities together

Program 2

Long-Term Recovery

Rebuilding stronger communities for generations to come.

Our Long-Term Work Includes:

  • Rebuilding homes
  • Donating construction tools
  • Restoring schools
  • Supporting long-term family sponsorship

We don't stop after the storm.

We stay until communities are whole again.

Program 3

Medical & Health Support

Strengthening healthcare access during and after disasters.

We Provide:

  • Medical supply shipments
  • Volunteer nurses, doctors, and medics
  • First aid kits
  • Support for local clinics
  • Medical logistics coordination

We make sure families not only survive — they stay healthy.
