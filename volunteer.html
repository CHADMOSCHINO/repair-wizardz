Ways to Help

Volunteer Roles

All skill levels welcome. Find the role that fits you best.

Sorting & Packing

Sort clothes, food donations, and pack emergency boxes for distribution.

Rebuilding Communities

Hands-on construction and repair work to rebuild damaged homes.

Landscaping & Repair

Clear debris, restore yards, and beautify affected neighborhoods.

Medical Support

Nurses, doctors, EMTs providing healthcare in affected areas.

Fundraising Ambassadors

Lead donation drives, campaigns, and community outreach.

On-Ground Team Leads

Coordinate distribution volunteers and logistics in Jamaica.

Distribution Volunteers

Deliver supplies directly to families in need across Jamaica.

Running Donation Drives

Organize local donation drives in your community.

Creative & Communications

Design, photography, social media, and storytelling support.
